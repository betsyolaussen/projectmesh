THE POTENTIAL PROBLEM

This project grew out of my own experiences with technology. Since I was a kid, I have been fascinated by technology, always desiring the latest and greatest. After five years working in Apple stores, and more in the tech support field in general, I have seen how technology can cause a wide range of emotions as well as cause divides among families and friends. I have seen parents and children fighting over which upgrades they are allowed, couples arguing under their breath about costs, or customers becoming far more rude and frustrated with employees than I usually see strangers become towards one another. However, I have also witnessed how technology can be the catalyst to forge new relationships, to bring people together. I see that in the way that I made many lifelong friends during my time at Apple, but on a broader scale I have seen the way that technology can bring people together across the country and world, a space to create community.

I hope that Project MESH can be a platform and gathering place for people in my community and beyond to shares their perspectives about the way that technology has shaped our communities and relationships in todayâ€™s world. I hope to create an ongoing dialogue where we can all help each other to be more mindful about how technology is impacting us, and to be more mindful in our relationships in general.
